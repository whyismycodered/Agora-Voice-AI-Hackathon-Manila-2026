# **App Name**: CivicPilot AI

## Core Features:

- AI Caseworker: Utilize a large language model to provide guidance on Philippine government services based on user queries.
- Passport Assistance: Provide information and guidance related to DFA passport services.
- Social Security Guidance: Offer assistance with SSS (Social Security System) inquiries and processes.
- Healthcare Support: Deliver information and support related to PhilHealth (Philippine Health Insurance Corporation) services.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#94B3FD), evoking trust and accessibility, in line with the Agora.io style. This color is reminiscent of the 'Agora' blue from the image, and will contrast with a white background.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F4FF), subtly desaturated and bright to maintain a clean, light theme. This background is of similar hue as the primary, but much lighter and more desaturated.
- Accent color: Light purple (#B894FD), analogous to the primary blue, to add a touch of vibrancy and draw attention to interactive elements such as CTAs, emulating the Agora.io branding gradient.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' for a contemporary, precise feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', to promote legibility.
- Use lucide-react icons for placeholders related to government services (passport, SSS, health).
- Ensure a clean, minimalist layout with centered alignment for the hero section and service cards.
- Subtle hover effects (scaling) on service cards to enhance interactivity.
